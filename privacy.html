Privacy Policy

How this private internal app handles data and authentication.
Home · Terms

Scope

This policy applies to the private/internal use of the application by authorized users only. It does not apply to external users or public deployments.

Data Collected

We collect the minimum necessary data to operate authentication and core features:

  • Account identifiers (internal user ID, email or username where applicable)
  • Authentication tokens and session metadata
  • Operational logs required for debugging and security

Use of Data

Data is used strictly for:

  • User authentication and authorization
  • Providing core features of the internal application
  • Ensuring security, integrity, and reliability

Storage & Retention

Data is stored in secure systems restricted to internal administrators. Retention is limited to operational needs and legal requirements. Logs may be rotated periodically.

Third‑Party Services

Integrations (e.g., OAuth providers) are configured to minimize shared data and adhere to provider policies. Tokens are stored securely and never exposed to unauthorized parties.

Access Control

Access is role‑based and limited to authorized internal users. Administrative access is audited and restricted.

Your Rights

As an internal user, you may request review or deletion of your data within organizational policy. Contact the administrator listed below.

Contact

For questions or requests, contact: admin@example.com
